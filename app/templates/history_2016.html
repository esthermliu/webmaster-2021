Present Day

2016 - 2021

Today, Neomedica continues to strive for its vision: a world where anyone can access affordable, world-class healthcare from the safety and comfort of their home. Our award-winning virtual reality care system has helped thousands of customers safely engage in physical therapy, phobia therapy, and other mental health services.

Following the COVID-19 (Coronavirus) pandemic, many Americans turned to Neomedica for diagnoses, symptom checks, return-to-work solutions, tests, and vaccines. In our characteristically humanitarian nature, we offered all these services at little to zero cost. Neomedica proudly became accredited by the NCQA & ACHC and has been featured on NBC, New York Times, USA Today, and many more networks.
