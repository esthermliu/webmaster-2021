Our providers

Neomedica partners with some of the world’s most prestigious developers, employers, hospitals, and health systems to offer industry-leading virtual treatment. Just like any other healthcare provided, you can expect that every provider is credentialed, accredited, highly qualified and working their finest to provide every patient with swift and effective care.

Meet the team

Physicians

Our physicians are medically trained, U.S. board certified in their fields, and licensed and credentialed. Neomedica physicians hold bachelor’s (B.A. or B.S.) Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) or Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine (D.O.) degrees.

Meet us!

Nurse Practitioners

Our nurse practitioners are medically trained, ANCC or AANP accredited, registered, and credentialed in their fields. Neomedica nurse practitioners hold Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) and Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) degrees.

Meet us!

Physician Assistants

Our physician assistants are medically trained, NCCPA certified, licensed and credentialed. MeMD physician assistants hold bachelor’s (B.A. or B.S.) and Physician Assistant Studies (MPAS), Health Science (MHS) or Medical Science (MMSc) degrees.

Meet us!

Therapists

Our behavioral health team of masters-level professionals includes experienced and accredited Licensed Professional Counselors, Licensed Clinical Social Workers, Marriage and Family Therapists and equivalent licensed professionals.
