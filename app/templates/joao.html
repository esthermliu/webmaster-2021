About / Leadership / Joao Santos

Overview

Joao Santos is Neomedica’s CFO, hailing from Sao Paulo, Brazil. The hardships and rewards of immigration from Brazil to the United States taught Joao about the importance of perseverance, adaptation, and never forgetting your roots.

With great numbers comes great responsibility.

— Joao Santos —

Early Life

Dubbed the “math genius” at age 4, Joao would amaze his teachers with his incredible mental math abilities. With the mind of a calculator, he quickly rose above the ranks and started the first math tutoring program in his Sao Paulo neighborhood in order to help other children in his community fall in love with the field of mathematics.

University Days

Carrying on his love for math, Joao went on to study applied mathematics at the University of Sao Paulo where he began taking economics courses, finding interest in the subject. He eventually transitioned his purely mathematical focus into an economic and financial perspective, as president of Sao Paulo’s Investment Club, an organization that still exists today. He was commencement speaker at his graduation in 1988. The same year, he began his post-graduation studies at Harvard University.

Chasing a Dream

Joao says that getting the chance to study at Harvard changed the course of his life. Overnight, he immigrated to the United States with nothing but a suitcase and a dream. Joao experienced the hardships of being an immigrant and a student at a prestigious institution. He learned how to speak English, how to navigate the trials and tribulations of moving to a new country. Joao met Sammy at a startup fundraising mixer hosted at Harvard. They shared a passion for fresh starts, entrepreneurship, and helping others less fortunate than themselves.
