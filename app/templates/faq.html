We at Neomedica make the most out of telemedicine and telehealth. To curb physical issues such as injury, aches and pains, and soreness, your therapy will involve relaxing, soothing physical exercises that your therapist will guide you through – these are conducted over video calls or virtual reality if you choose. To address medical issues like nausea, diarrhea, cold, flu, headaches, or any other such problem, you may virtually visit a physician for an in-depth medical appointment or diagnosis.
