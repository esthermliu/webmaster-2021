What are Therapy Rabbits?

Therapy cats are trained rabbits that aid humans in emotional support through interaction. Rabbit therapy, although uncommon, is often practiced in senior care homes, rehab centers, detention centers, and a number of other areas. They can help prevent a variety of types of suffering, including depression, anxiety, and phobias. Neomedica can refer you to trained, professionally certified, and trustworthy therapy Rabbits. The Lulling Power of Therapy Rabbits

Scientists have long accredited the therapeutic powers of animals with oxytocin, the “love hormone,” responsible for alleviating stress and anxiety. Moreover, in Japan, researchers have even found that mutual gazing between a human and an animal can reduce stress by causing oxytocin levels to surge. If you are recovering from a mental or physical condition, this type of therapy might be perfect for you.
