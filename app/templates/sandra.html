About / Leadership / Sandra Nguyen

Overview

Uplifting and supporting voices that may not be heard is Sandra’s number one goal. As a participant in Women in Business panels in her area, Sandra’s background in business brings a key perspective to Neomedica.

I strive to uphold Neomedica’s values of equity and inclusivity each day.

— Sandra Nguyen —

Early Life

Growing up in sunny California with five older brothers, Sandra constantly had to fight for a seat at the table, both physically and metaphorically. With a keen interest in business, Sandra organized bake sales at her middle school to fundraise for school events.

University Days

Sandra went on to study communications at the University of California, Berkeley. Her first ever friend in college was her freshman year roommate, Sammy Urtzal. Learning from how difficult it was to have her voice heard in a male dominated household, Sandra knew she wanted to help other women find their voice in business. She joined the UC Berkeley Women in Business Symposium to encourage more women to pursue entrepreneurship.

Chasing a Dream

Sandra was one of Sammy’s first supporters in the Neomedica mission. Her background in communications work came in handy, and Sammy asked her to be the team’s CCO. Sandra gladly accepted this role, and continues to make Neomedica and the healthcare field a more vibrant industry.
