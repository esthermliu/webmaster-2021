Augmented and Virtual Reality’s place in telehealth is growing consistently. Pet Therapy Solutions

Pet therapy is a relaxing, guided interaction between a person and a trained animal. It is an excellent, safe, and satisfying coping technique that helps tremendously with recovering or coping with a health problem or mental disorder. At Neomedica, we offer dogs, cats, birds, horses, rabbits, and reptiles for rent as therapy animals. Note: Neomedica requires background checks and waivers to rent animals.
