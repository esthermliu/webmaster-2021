Our Approach to Occupational Therapy

At Neomedica, we take occupational therapy very seriously. For users exploring occupational therapy for the first time, read on to gain a basic understanding of what it is. The general premise of occupational therapy is to help people of all ages, situations, and backgrounds participate in the things they want and need to do via therapeutic exercises involving ‘occupations’, or daily activities. Such interventions might include helping disabled kids participate fully in social situations, helping recovering PTSD victims reintegrate into daily life, and supporting veteran adults that experience cognitive changes.

Cognitive-Based Therapy

Although occupational therapy deals largely with physical and motor skills, Neomedica believes in applying it beyond the physical scope. We help people of all ages reestablish proper cognitive functions through comprehensive, progressively challenging exercises and tests. By scheduling a video call with one of our experienced therapists (see below for more information), you can re-experience the fluidity of proper cognitive ability.
