Our NCQA & ACHC Accreditation

What does it mean to be accredited by NCQA?

The National Committee for Quality Assurance, abbreviated NCQA, is a nonprofit organization that sets standards on the quality of healthcare. The NCQA measures the quality of a health plan through several different metrics, including ensuring that a company provides the right care, the right amount of care, and timely care. To be accredited by the NCQA, a company must not only meet these base quality standards, but also demonstrate that their plan expands upon and improves existing industry standards of care.

After completing a thorough, year-long assessment of our plan, the NCQA determined that we meet and exceed all standards and thus have accredited us accordingly.
