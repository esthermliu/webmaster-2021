Unions

Employees deserve the right wages. Employees deserve a healthy workplace. Employees deserve a say in decisions that relate to them. That’s why Neomedica supports our employees’ unions. We recognize the power of a health workplace environment; if our doctors, nurses, therapists, and psychiatrists aren’t happy, they can’t do their best work. If they can’t do their best work, our patients won’t get the treatment they need and deserve. If you are a Neomedica employee, contact us today to learn about your union options.

Mental Health Work

Neomedica works with several associations to promote mental health issues and self-care methods. Mental health is very important, and following the pandemic, it has become more apparent to many people whose lives slowed down. As such, our specialized therapists work with mental health advocacy organizations, such as Mental Health America, to promote and share information about mental health issues, and connect with everyday people to understand and assist them in their mental health struggles. Contact us today to learn more!

Telehealth Work

Neomedica also works with several associations that promote telehealth. One part of making healthcare accessible to everyone is making Neomedica known to as many people as possible. In fact, our internal study found that around 70% of those who learnt about telehealth through one of these associations attended at least one appointment with a Neomedica specialist. Contact us today to learn more about how you can help spread the word about telehealth!
