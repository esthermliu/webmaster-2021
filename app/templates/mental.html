Uses

VR therapy uses a non-intrusive visual immersion technology to create an interactable artificial environment, allowing for a doctors to monitor patients and for patients to interact with things without any fear. It has been used in conjunction with cognitive behavior therapy and has been proven to work effectively for a multitude of disorders including panic disorder, body image disturbances, binge eating disorders, fear of flying and far more.
