About / Leadership / Callum Hodge

Overview

An experienced technician and data scientist with an engineering background, Callum Hodge has served as Neomedica’s Chief Information Officer since 2013. With decades of healthcare industry experience collaborating with leaders, stakeholders and patients alike, Callum brings an unprecedented level of experience and perspective to the Neomedica executive team.

Harnessing information has the potential to change lives.

— Callum Hodge —

Early Life

As a young lad growing up on the outskirts of Sheffield, UK, Callum had two priorities in life: kicking a ball around with his mates and tinkering with just about any electronic device he could. After returning home from a long day of football (soccer), he would sit himself down at his father’s workstation to work with circuits, often forgetting to complete his schoolwork.

University Days

Luckily, Callum’s sharp wit and work ethic landed him admission in the prestigious Cambridge University’s School of Engineering to study Electrical Engineering. While Callum was often holed up in his dormitory poring over his projects, he could never say no to a game of football with his friends!

Chasing a Dream

Callum went on to work in industry for nearly 15 years after his graduation for well-established health care companies in the United Kingdom. Tired of routine with big name companies and their bureaucracies, Callum was on the hunt for a cutting edge, bold work environment. He stumbled across a job posting by Sammy, and immediately knew Neomedica was the place for him.
