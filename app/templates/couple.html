Imago Relationship Therapy

Imago Relationship Theory was developed in 1980 by Dr. Harville Hendrix and Dr. Helen LaKelly Hunt. It operates on the principle of entirely converting conflict into personal growth and healing through relational connection. Our therapists are specially and extensively trained to best provide this type of therapy to you and your loved one. Neomedica strongly believes that strong-standing relationships are key to keeping both you and your partner happy and intimately connected.
