Why work here

Mission-driven Organization

At Neomedica, we have a clear mission: provide accessible and affordable healthcare to everyone, all from the comfort of home. We believe in patients over profits, in widespread services over selective ones. Everything we do is centered around these principles, and if you believe in them, you’ll fit right into our team that works to make it possible everyday.

Innovative Culture

Neomedica’s vision for a better system of healthcare is unprecedented. It’s far beyond the realm of our current system, and it requires a creative and constantly evolving process. For us, innovation isn’t a choice; it’s an inherent part of our culture. We value a wide range of perspectives, and place value on ideas outside of the box.

Breadth of Opportunities

At Neomedica, we have to move fast. To provide the best quality of service to our patients, we must adapt on the go and come up with creative solutions. As a result, we have a large variety of opportunities available to our employees to grow their skills and gain valuable experience.

Great Benefits

Even though we aim to make healthcare affordable, that doesn’t mean that we skimp on benefits for our valued staff! You’ll receive free health insurance and healthcare (from Neomedica, of course!), as well as flexible hours and wages. If our patients love your work, we even guarantee a year-end bonus!

Medical Health Solutions

At Neomedica, we’re on the front lines of advancements in medical health. We routinely develop and set new industry standards regarding care and methods of treatment for a variety of illnesses that affect our patients. If you’d like to be a part of this effort, Neomedica is the place for you!
