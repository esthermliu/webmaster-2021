About / Leadership / Paul Zhang

Overview

A little blood, sweat and tears never hurt anyone – especially not Paul Zhang. From his humble beginnings in San Francisco to the vibrant culture at Yale, Paul knows how to show up...and stand out. As Neomedica’s Chief Marketing Officer, Paul lets the world know how fantastic Neomedica’s services are, leading the marketing team in their quest to publicize Neomedica and improve their brand image.

Marketing bridges the gap between the product and the consumer.

— Sandra Nguyen —

Early Life

Paul grew up in the bustling Chinatown district of San Francisco, as his parents operated a family-owned restaurant. Life in the service industry was tough and busy, which developed Paul’s strong and hardworking character. Paul learned about marketing and advertising as a young child, when he would help his mother create promotional flyers to attract customers to their business.

University Days

Paul went on to study business at Yale University, but never forgot the humble roots he grew in the Bay Area. At Yale, he participated in debate conferences, marketing associations, and lead Yale’s first design club.

Chasing a Dream

Upon graduation, Paul knew the impact good marketing could have on a small business. He set out to find local startups, when he was introduced to Sammy at the very same mixer where she met Joao. After expressing his impressive passions and credentials, Sammy immediately snapped him up.
