Pioneers of health

Innovation: it's in our DNA

Future-oriented approach

With futuristic solutions like virtual reality and seamless video calling, Neomedica is built for the 21st century and beyond. We pioneer the solutions that the world and industry of telehealth follows.

Seamless meeting experience

With Neomedica’s secure, fast, and seamless video call viewer, you can join your appointment on any device, no matter where you’re located. Our beautifully designed scheduling and meeting system takes the stress of lag away.

Cross-platform access

Whether you’re joining from your mobile device, tablet, computer, or even Smart TV, Neomedica’s seamless platform will always remain constant. We believe that everyone deserves high-quality care, no matter the device it’s being accessed from.

Pay only when we help

Neomedica does not offer a stingy subscription fee like most other telemedicine services. Instead, we simply charge a flat fee for every appointment. Optionally, you can set your account to charge your card automatically. This way, you won’t be charged for any pesky hidden fees – with Neomedica, what you see is what you get: complete transparency.

For business

Bring Neomedica to your organization

Employer Groups

Choosing telemedicine as a more seamless, convenient group healthcare plan for your employees can boost morale and thus increase their productivity and longevity at your firm. Aside from the big savings, especially for self-insured employers, the added benefit of telemedicine will make your company more attractive to prospective employees. Finally, we provide 24/7 access to our seamless, world-class services for all employees.

Government and Education

When the pandemic hit, the CDC was racing to stop the spread. Immediately, several companies began intensive research into a potential vaccine; Neomedica became one of them. Out of all the companies, we were the most transparent, providing status updates and sharing all of our findings with the CDC. As a result, we’ve developed a strong partnership with the CDC, and we offer free CDC scientists free Neomedica care at any time.

Unions and Associations

Employees deserve the right wages. Employees deserve a healthy workplace. Employees deserve a say in decisions that relate to them. That’s why Neomedica supports our employees’ unions. We recognize the power of a health workplace environment; if our doctors, nurses, therapists, and psychiatrists aren’t happy, they can’t do their best work. If they can’t do their best work, our patients won’t get the treatment they need and deserve. If you are a Neomedica employee, contact us today to learn about your union options.
