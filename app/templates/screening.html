Treatment

If you’re stuck at a bed at home or a hospital, Neomedica is here to help. We provide 24/7 calling for any problems that may arise, we’re even here if you just need someone to talk to. If you are isolating at home with Covid-19, we can monitor your symptoms and provide expert guidance – such as telling you when you are good to go, and when you may need additional care. By maintaining continuity of care, permanent negative consequences are prevented by the maximum extent possible.
