Pay how you want

Easy Payment

Pay with any type of card; credit, debit, or an HSA card – we accept American Express, Visa, Discover, and MasterCard. Simply enter your card information and turn on AutoPay, and we will automatically charge you whenever you visit. We protect your privacy by holding this information separately from your health information.
