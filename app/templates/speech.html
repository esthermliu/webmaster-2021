Why Speech Therapy?

If you have a tough time articulating exactly what you want to because of a speech impairment, you may have either a speech or language disorder. Our speech professionals are trained to recognize and treat many different types of disorders, listed below:

  • Articulation disorders
  • Fluency disorders
  • Resonance disorders
  • Receptive disorders
  • Expressive disorders
  • Cognitive-communication disorders
  • Aphasia
  • Dysarthria

Proven Speech Therapy Techniques

Neomedica believes in a set of tried and trusted speech therapy techniques help both you and our therapists have the smoothest treatment process possible. Depending on your diagnosis, your therapist will walk you through the list of options for treatment that you have, and you’ll be able to customize your therapy and set a flexible schedule that works for you. Not only are our therapists specially trained on a core set of techniques, which are listed below, but they quickly learn and adapt to a patient’s therapy needs with alternative methods as well.

  • Articulation Therapy
  • Language Intervention Therapy
  • Oral Motor Therapy
  • Lee Silverman Speech Therapy (LSVT)
  • VitalStim Therapy

Children’s Speech Therapy

If your child is experiencing a speech delay, our team of speech therapists at Neomedica are here to help. Depending on the exact type of speech condition your child is experiencing, we will present you with a list of therapy options, including what techniques we can try to administer and whether your child is with a group of similar children or by themself. We’re here to work with you and your child and help them build courage and gain confidence through fluent speech.

Adult Speech Therapy

If you are experiencing problems with your speech that you didn’t used to have before, Neomedica can help. Whether you recently suffered a stroke, an injury, or nothing at all, our specialists can quickly diagnose and treat a range of conditions. You’ll have full control over both the treatment method and a large selection of specialists to choose from to best serve your needs. Regardless of what the problem is, Neomedica is here to help you get through it, and get back your confidence with speech.
