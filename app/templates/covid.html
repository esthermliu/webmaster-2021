COVID-19 Testing

After the hectic year that was 2020, there’s not a lot to be sure about. The virus turned our lives upside down, and just as we thought that cases were trending down and we would be able to reopen, they spiked. It can be frustrating, especially as someone who always does their part to wash their hands, wear a mask, and socially distance as much as possible.

Neomedica realized this sentiment, and we acted. Our high-quality testing program ensures that you’ll be able to get a test quickly and reliably, all from the comfort of your home. Simply request a kit on our website and we will mail it to your address in 3 days or less! You’ll always be able to view your results 3 days after we process your test. If you test positive, Neomedica is still here to help; we provide screening and treatment options as well!
